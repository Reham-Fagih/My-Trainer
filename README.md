## Overview

My Trainer is a mobile fitness application that makes personalized health guidance accessible to everyone. The platform leverages Artificial Intelligence to analyze body composition from a single photo and generate customized workout and nutrition plans tailored to each user's fitness goals.

The application combines health tracking with gamification features to keep users motivated and engaged throughout their fitness journey.

## Features

- AI-based body fat analysis from a single photo
- Personalized weekly workout plans
- Personalized nutrition plans with macro calculations
- Points and rewards system
- Leaderboard for competitive motivation
- User authentication and secure profile management
- Progress tracking and performance monitoring
